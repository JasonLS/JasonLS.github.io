My interests

Gaming

My favorite pastime growing up is gaming. While most see it has childish and a waste of time, I discovered the benefits of it on my childhood. One of the core mechanics in most games are problem solving. The player is given a problem and they have to break it down, look at what they have, make a plan and solve the problem then move on to the next. This is a skill i think most people lack when dealing with problems. They take off more then they can chew and rush to meet deadlines instead of proper planning.
